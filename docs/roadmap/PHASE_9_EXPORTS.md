# PHASE 9 — EXPORTS & REPORTING

## Objective

Implement engineering exports and reporting capabilities.

---

# Goals

- documentation exports
- PDF generation
- traceability matrices
- estimation reports

---

# Export Types

Support:
- requirements documentation
- engineering reports
- traceability reports
- estimation summaries

---

# Technical Requirements

Exports should:
- support structured formatting
- support reusable templates
- support future branding/theming

---

# Acceptance Criteria

- Reports generate successfully
- PDFs export correctly
- Traceability matrices generate correctly
